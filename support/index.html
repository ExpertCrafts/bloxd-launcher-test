General Inquiries

Q: Why are there so few features?
A: We currently have a dedicated team of two active developers who are also managing school and professional obligations, which limits our capacity to expand features at this time.
Q: I am not receiving updates communicated on the Discord server. What steps should I take?
A: We recommend clearing your browser cache, as the process may vary depending on your browser. For assistance, please refer to online resources specific to your browser.
Q: Is Facet/Bloxd available for free?
A: Yes, Facet/Bloxd is completely free to use. We appreciate any support through our donation links, as contributions help us continue our work. You can also upgrade to Pro for additional features and ad-free experience.

Technical Issues

Q: How does one report a bug?
A: Bugs can be reported via our Discord server or by submitting an issue on our GitHub repository. Please provide detailed information regarding the encountered issue.
Q: What may be causing the crashes I am experiencing?
A: Crashes may result from insufficient system resources, software conflicts, or application bugs. We recommend updating to the latest version and ensuring your system meets the necessary requirements.
Q: I cannot access certain features in the app.
A: Some features may be restricted based on your account type. Check your account status in the settings menu of the app. Free users have access to basic features, while Pro users enjoy additional benefits and ad-free experience.

Account & Pro Features

Q: How do I sign in to my account?
A: Click on the profile section in the bottom left of the app interface. Then click the "Sign In" button and follow the prompts to authenticate with your Puter account.
Q: What are the benefits of upgrading to Pro?
A: Pro users enjoy ad-free experience, exclusive features, priority support, and early access to new functionality. The right sidebar ads are removed for Pro users, and you get additional customization options.
Q: How do I upgrade to Pro?
A: After signing in, click on the profile menu (gear icon) and select the upgrade options. You can choose from various Pro plans including temporary and permanent options. You can also use redemption codes if available.
Q: What is the difference between Pro and YT Pro?
A: YT Pro is a special account type for content creators. Both Pro and YT Pro accounts enjoy the same features, but YT Pro is specifically for YouTubers and content creators who promote our platform.

Workshop Concerns

Q: The texture packs are not loading as expected.
A: Our file loading process utilizes the GitHub API, which may experience latency. We appreciate your patience, and if the issue persists, please refer to the Technical Issues section to report the bug.
Q: I am unable to download the texture packs, or the downloaded file appears to be corrupted.
A: Downloading files is managed through the GitHub API, which can sometimes be slow. We thank you for your understanding. If the problem continues, please refer to the Technical Issues section to report the issue.
Q: Why is the preview not loading?
A: The preview utilizes Three.js to render textures from a downloaded texture file. File downloads are managed via the GitHub API, which may occasionally experience delays. We appreciate your patience. If the issue persists, please consult the Technical Issues section to report the problem.
Q: What data is collected when I comment on a texture pack?
A: Our commenting system is powered by Disqus. For more details on data collection, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

Creator Concerns

Q: I don't know how to use the UI.
A: The Creator section provides tools for making texture packs and other content. You can access various services like Piskel for sprite creation, Blockbench for 3D modeling, and other tools via the Creator interface. You can also click the "Show Walkthrough" button in your profile menu to get started.
Q: The webpages in the opened windows aren't loading.
A: We utilize external services in the Creator and cannot guarantee the uninterrupted operation of any site. If one or more sites aren't loading, please be patient as we await their return to service.

Wiki Concerns

Q: The wiki is not loading as expected.
A: Similar to our other services, our file loading process relies on the GitHub API, which may experience latency. We kindly request your patience, and if the issue persists, please refer to the Technical Issues section to report the bug.

Support Us

Q: Can I support the developers?
A: Absolutely! Feel free to join our Discord and consider upgrading to Pro to support our efforts. You can also contribute by reporting bugs, suggesting features, or sharing the platform with others.
Q: How can I contribute to the project?
A: You can contribute by reporting bugs, suggesting features, creating content for the Workshop, or assisting with documentation through our GitHub repository or Discord server.
